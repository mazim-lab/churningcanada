Frequently asked questions
Why should you apply for a TD Aeroplan Credit Card?

Whether you like to travel the world or love to shop, TD has credit cards with all the features and benefits you can enjoy. You can earn Aeroplan points on every eligible purchase and can earn double the points at partner retailers via the Aeroplan eStore5. Redeeming your earned Aeroplan points is easy on aircanada.com with flights, hotel rooms, car rentals, merchandise, gift cards and more as your redemption options.

What other benefits do the TD Aeroplan Credit Cards have?
The everyday purchases you already make earn Aeroplan points that can be redeemed towards your next vacation.
Your Card can provide collision coverage for car rentals10.
Your card gives you a suite of insurance coverages when you travel11.
Your card comes with features to help increase security, to give you more confidence using your card.
Earn 50% more Aeroplan points and 50% more Stars at participating Starbucks® stores. Simply link your TD Aeroplan Visa Card with your Starbucks® Rewards account to start earning. Conditions apply. Learn more.
How can you earn Aeroplan points with TD?

There are lots of ways to earn Aeroplan points on eligible purchases made with your TD® Aeroplan® Visa Credit Card.

You can earn Aeroplan points on all your eligible purchases, and you can earn even more on additional purchase categories!
Get Welcome Bonus Aeroplan points with any TD® Aeroplan® Visa Credit Card9. Conditions apply.
Earn Aeroplan points twice when you pay with your Card and provide your Aeroplan number at over 150 partner brands and at 200+ online retailers via the Aeroplan eStore5.
Visit the Aeroplan website to find out about the latest offers from Aeroplan partners.
Your Aeroplan points will not expire as long as you are a TD Aeroplan Cardholder Primary Cardholder14.
How can you redeem your Aeroplan points?
Every seat, every flight, no restrictions
Every Air Canada seat available to buy for cash is also available for points – no restrictions, no blackout periods.
Use Aeroplan points for more than flights
Redeem your points across your trip, from hotel rooms to car rentals.
Use Aeroplan points for life’s necessities
Aeroplan isn’t just about travel. Use your points for merchandise, experiences and gift certificates.
