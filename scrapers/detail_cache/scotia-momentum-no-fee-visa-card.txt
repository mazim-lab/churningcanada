                    How do I redeem my Cash Back balance?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    Primary and co-borrower cardholders can redeem a Cash Back balance of $25 or greater anytime: 

Visit your credit card account overview
Select Redeem
Pick an account to receive your Cash Back
Enter an amount 
Select Redeem, review your details, and then Confirm

If your balance is less than $25, you’ll have a progress bar showing the amount left before you can redeem your Cash Back.



    



    

        
            
            
                
                    Does my Cash Back balance expire?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    Your Cash Back balance will not expire while your credit card account is open. 
 
If you close your Scotia Momentum credit card account, you have 90 days from the close date to redeem your Cash Back balance. 



    



    

        
            
            
                
                    Why can’t I deposit my Cash Back into some accounts?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    You might have accounts that aren’t eligible for you to deposit your Cash Back balance.
 
For example, if you have a credit card account with you and another co-borrower, you won’t be able to redeem the Cash Back into a non-joint bank account, such as a sole chequing account or operating account. 
  
Alternatively, if you’re the only primary borrower of your credit card account, you won’t be able to redeem the Cash Back into a joint account, such as a joint chequing account or operating account that you have with another accountholder. 
 
If you have a cash back redemption in progress, it may take approximately 1 to 2 business days before we can process a redemption to a different account. 



    



    

        
            
            
                
                    Why is my earned cash back not available for redemption?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    There are a few reasons your Cash Back balance may not be available to redeem. 

You haven’t reached the $25 redemption minimum yet. 

The bonus portion of the Cash Back earned as part of an offer isn’t available to redeem yet.  

For example, the bonus portion of Cash Back earned related to the Scotia Momentum® Visa Infinite welcome bonus will be displayed on your statements but will become available to redeem online after the promotion period has ended.


You aren’t the primary cardholder or co-borrower on the credit card account. Supplementary cardholders aren’t eligible to redeem the Cash Back balance. 

Your account is closed. Call the number on the back of your card within 90 days of the close date to redeem your outstanding Cash Back balance. 

Your account won’t be eligible to redeem Cash Back if it is not in good standing.  

Anytime Cash Back redemption is currently available for Scotia Momentum Visa Cash Back cardholders.




    



    

        
            
            
                
                    What does it mean to be in good standing?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    Your credit card account is in good standing when these conditions are met:

Your account is open
Your account is not past due, including that we have received your total minimum payment by the payment due date
All cardholders are in compliance with the credit agreement that applies to your account

Review your credit agreement (the Revolving Credit Agreement) and the Cash Back or Rewards Program Terms that apply to your account for more details about good standing.
