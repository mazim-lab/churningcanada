                    Where can I learn more about credit?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    ScotiaAdvice+ is a great place to learn more about credit, with guides on credit reports, credit scores, and credit monitoring, plus tips on how to improve your credit score.

If you’re registered for TransUnion Credit Services through Scotiabank, you can check your credit score and report by signing in to your account online or using the mobile banking app.



    



    

        
            
            
                
                    How is my credit score calculated?
                    
                
            
        
        
        

    TransUnion calculates your credit score based on a variety of factors in your credit history, such as:

Your payment history for your loans and credit cards
How much you owe on your credit accounts
How long your accounts have been open
Your credit mix (e.g., credit cards, mortgages, lines of credit)
How much credit you use and have available
How often and how recently you’ve applied for credit

Visit our credit scores page on Advice+ to learn more about what impacts your score.
